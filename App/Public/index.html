Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully.

Introduction:

Thank you for participating in our study. In this experiment, your payment will depend on the toss of a coin. Your task is to decide how much money you want to receive if the coin comes up heads and if the coin comes up tails.


Task Overview:

There will be a total of 25 rounds. For each round, you will do the following:

  • Determine how much money you want to receive if the coin comes up heads or tails.
  • Mark your chosen point on the line that represents your preferred combination of payments. You can do this by clicking or tapping on the line.
  • There are no right or wrong answers; your choices are entirely up to you.
